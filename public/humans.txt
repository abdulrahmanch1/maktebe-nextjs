/* DESCRIPTION */
Dar Al-Qurra is a free Arabic digital library
providing thousands of books without registration
or advertisements. Features include a built-in
PDF reader, AI assistant, advanced notes, and
offline reading support.

Mission: Democratize access to Arabic literature
and knowledge for everyone, everywhere.

Vision: Become the most comprehensive and
user-friendly Arabic digital library globally.
